Welcome to Palawan

Palawan is the Philippines' last frontier, a paradise of pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and untouched natural beauty.

Top Attractions

Puerto Princesa Underground River

One of the New 7 Wonders of Nature, this river is a must-see. Explore the stunning subterranean river and marvel at its intricate cave formations.

El Nido

Famous for its white-sand beaches, limestone cliffs, and coral reefs, El Nido offers breathtaking views and unforgettable island-hopping experiences.

Coron

Known for its WWII-era wreck dive sites, coral gardens, and turquoise lakes, Coron is a diver's paradise and a haven for underwater adventurers.

Things to Do in Palawan

Kayaking

Explore the hidden lagoons and secret beaches of Palawan by kayak. It's a great way to get up close with nature and discover the island's serene beauty.

Snorkeling

Dive into the crystal-clear waters and discover the vibrant underwater world of Palawan. Snorkeling is a must-do activity for marine life enthusiasts.

Hiking

Embark on a hiking adventure through Palawan's lush rainforests. The trails offer stunning views, diverse wildlife, and a chance to connect with nature.

Island Hopping

Set sail on an island-hopping tour and visit some of Palawan's most beautiful and remote islands. Each island has its own unique charm and attractions.
